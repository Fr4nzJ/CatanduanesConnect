About Catanduanes Connect

Catanduanes Connect is an AI-powered business directory and job portal designed to connect the people of Catanduanes with local businesses, service providers, and job opportunities. Our mission is to boost local economic development, make services more accessible, and support businesses in reaching customers and employees.
